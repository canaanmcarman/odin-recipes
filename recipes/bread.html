Basic Rustic Loaf

Easy to make at home Basic Rustic Loaf!

Ingredients
  • Yeast
  • White Bread Flour
  • Salt
  • Water
Steps
  1. Combine Ingredients (sans oil) in large mixin bowl until comes together, wait 15 mins
  2. Fold over into ball shape, wait 20 mins. Repeat 2 more times
  3. Rest for 2 hours. 1 hour into rest preheat oven to 475f
  4. Fold dough into oval shape and put into bread pan, put in oven for 25 mins or until bread is nice deep brown
